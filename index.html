FAQ's

1. What is the essence of this workshop? When and how long is it?

Our aim is to blend fun with learning and to enlighten the audience about the greatness of our country.

2. Who are the speakers?

Eminent speakers who are experts in their subject matter will share their knowledge enriched with experience connecting it to the Generation next.

3. Who can Attend?

This is for girls/ladies aged between 8yrs to 60yrs respecting the traditional and dharmic way of life and are keen to know the answers for many of their questions. However they will be properly segregated based on age so that a homogeneous group is formed.

4. What are the eligibility criteria?

There is no prerequisite as such but for the open mind to learn and the urge to continue the learning and apply them in day today life.

5. What is the venue and Does it Include boarding and lodging?

Venue:  SRDF Vivekananda Vidhyalaya , Chrompet. It has good ventilation. Separate halls for different age groups. Sufficient bathroom/rest room facilities and ample open space for group activity and games. It is located in the residential area with fully secured gate. In addition to that security people will be deployed to ensure safe and peaceful stay. Simple and hygienic vegetarian food will be provided for entire two days.

6. Whether transportation will be provided?

Participants should arrange for their transport. However planning to create a whats app group area wise based on the registration where a volunteer shall guide you through.

7.Is it compulsory to stay back at venue or can I go and come back?

Night stay facilities are available. However, if one wishes to go home after the day1 event (which may go upto 7.30 p.m )shall report on the next day 10 minutes before the scheduled time.

8.What are the post workshop plans?

In order to keep the spirit high and to continue the knowledge gain we are planning to have a weekend online classes followed by monthly once meeting where most of the workshop topics will be dealt in detail. A complete syllabus for 15 months will be shared later.

9.Is there any dress code

Yes. Being peak summer, cotton casual wear with dhuppatta/stole shall be the comfortable one. Since we have an exercise session on both days we recommend a cotton kurta pyjama for that.

10.Is it open to Chennai Alone?

All are welcome across the country who has got the flair to introspect, reflect and wish to be a change agent.

11.What all we need to bring?

Upon registration you will be provided with the link along with the confirmation mail, which gives you all the necessary input.

12.Is there any fee?

A nominal fee of Rs.500 per person including the boarding and lodging.

13. How and to whom can I pay my registration fees?

Upon registration the organizer will call you in person to confirm your details and also give you the details regarding the mode of payment.

14.I have a boy can I bring him?

Yes if he is less than 8 years with a minimal charge of 200.

From ancient times, Hinduism has bestowed upon women a social status of dignity and honor. Women in Sanathana Dharma had additional responsibility in understanding Vaideeka Dharma & cascading the knowledge to the subsequent generations. In the last several years, our country has undergone significant changes in its social and economic environments. These changes have altered our traditional family structure, learning and also the role of women within the family and society. Our community was quick to adopt & adapt to the changing environment. While embracing the new opportunities, our modern day women should not lose sight of their roots. They have to learn the nuances of balancing traditions and modern day lifestyle. It is our endeavor, therefore to provide you with an opportunity to be aware of the sublime values of life, tradition and culture as sanctioned by our Vedas and Scriptures. We are starting this journey with weekly online class, and once in a month meet-up in your respective area. This online class will be a turning point in your life. This will change your perception and the way you think. It will make you become a responsible leader, a change agent, become duty bound while handling the rights responsibly. A woman enlightened, Enlightens the whole generation. Come, Join, Enrich and Enjoy.
